## **Zoea V2 Feature Brainstorm (Super App Foundation)**

### **1\. Core Modules**

* **Listings Engine**: Unified system for all categories — events, restaurants, bars, coffee shops, hotels, tour packages, shops, and general services.

* **Profiles**:

  * **User Profiles**: Saved favorites, bookings, purchases, and reviews.

  * **Merchant Profiles**: Description, gallery, contact info, categories, location, and reviews.

* **Bookings & Orders**:

  * Real-time booking for **hotels** (rooms), events, and services.

  * Product ordering and checkout for shops, restaurants, and tours.

  * Integrated availability management and calendar view.

  * Booking confirmations, reschedules, and cancellations.

* **Payments Integration**:

  * Mobile money (MoMo, Airtel), IremboPay, and card payments.

  * Secure checkout for orders and bookings.

  * Split payments for groups or shared bookings.

#### **Hotel Reservations (Booking.com–style)**

* **Search & Filters**: Date range, guests/rooms, price range, star rating, amenities, neighborhood.

* **Room Types & Rate Plans**: Room categories, occupancy rules, board types (RO/BB/HB/All-inclusive), refundable vs non-refundable.

* **Inventory & Restrictions**: Per-day inventory, min/max stay, closed to arrival/departure, blackout dates.

* **Availability Calendar**: Monthly/weekly calendar for hotels \+ real-time availability for users.

* **Policies**: Cancellation windows, deposits, no-show rules, pre-auth/hold, taxes & fees breakdown.

* **Payments**: Pay now, pay at property, or deposit; MoMo/Airtel/cards supported; invoices/receipts.

* **Guest Journey**: Special requests, arrival time, add-ons (airport pickup, breakfast, late checkout), digital vouchers.

* **Partner Extranet**: Photos, amenities, policies, pricing, bulk rate updates, closures.

* **Integrations (Phase 2+)**: Channel manager/PMS (e.g., SiteMinder/Cloudbeds), iCal, room sync, web booking widget.

* **Post-Stay**: Verified reviews, itemized receipts, loyalty credit.

### **2\. Discovery & Exploration**

* **Nearby Mode**: Users can view **events, restaurants, bars, hotels, and venues near their current location** using GPS.

* **Interactive Map View**: Map pins show venues and events with distance indicators.

* **Smart Search**: Search by **city, district, or specific neighborhood**.

* **Filters & Sorting**: By distance, rating, popularity, open/closed status, or trending.

* **Explore Tab**: Combines categories with location filters (“Events near me”, “Top cafés in Kimihurura”).

* **Curated Collections**: “Weekend Getaways”, “Best Deals Nearby”, “Popular Now in Kigali”.

* **Saved Locations**: Users can set default cities or frequently visited areas for personalized results.

### **3\. Merchant System**

* **Merchant Onboarding**: Businesses can register and verify their Zoea Merchant account.

* **Merchant Categories**: Hotels, Restaurants, Bars, Coffee Shops, Tour Operators, Shops, Services, and Experiences.

* **Product & Service Listings**:

  * Add items, packages, or experiences with images, pricing, and availability.

  * Support for variants (sizes, durations, add-ons).

* **Inventory & Booking Management**:

  * Track stock or available slots.

  * Manage reservations and orders in one dashboard.

* **Promotions & Deals**:

  * Flash sales, discounts, or campaign-based offers.

* **Analytics Panel**:

  * Insights on visits, orders, bookings, revenue, and customer trends.

### **4\. Zoea Channel Manager Module (Monetized)**

* **Unified Dashboard**: Manage hotel inventory, rates, and bookings from Zoea \+ external OTAs (Booking.com, Airbnb, Expedia).

* **Two-Way Sync**: Real-time sync for rooms, rates, and reservations.

* **Rate & Inventory Management**: Pricing rules, restrictions, and blackout control.

* **Reservation Feed**: Centralized list of all bookings with source tracking.

* **Revenue Analytics**: ADR, RevPAR, and channel performance stats.

* **Policy Controls**: Cancellation, deposits, and no-show management.

* **Automation**: Auto sync every few minutes or via webhooks.

* **Phase Rollout**:

  * Phase 1: Zoea-only property manager.

  * Phase 2: Add Booking.com \+ Airbnb sync.

  * Phase 3: Expand to Expedia \+ PMS.

  * Phase 4: Public API \+ SaaS monetization.

**Monetization Models:**

* Subscription: Monthly or annual hotel access plans.

* Commission: 1–2% on OTA-synced bookings.

* Premium Bundle: Part of Zoea Gold Business Plan.

* Integration/API Fees: For third-party usage.

### **5\. Agent Module (Affiliate & Onboarding Program)**

* **Agent Accounts**: Verified individuals or businesses that help onboard users and merchants.

* **Onboarding Tools**:

  * Register new users and merchants directly through agent dashboard or app.

  * Assign referral codes or QR invitations.

* **Tracking & Analytics**:

  * View signups, conversions, and active merchants linked to the agent.

  * Commission summary dashboard with filters (daily, weekly, monthly).

* **Reward System**:

  * Earn commissions on merchant subscriptions, bookings, or orders.

  * Tiered rewards based on activity and performance (e.g., Bronze → Gold → Platinum agents).

  * Instant MoMo payouts or redeemable Zoea Points.

* **Agent Leaderboard & Recognition**:

  * Public leaderboard for top agents.

  * Certification and special badges for verified and performing agents.

* **Monetization & Growth**:

  * Reduces Zoea acquisition costs while creating a scalable field-sales network.

  * Encourages offline-to-online adoption across Rwanda’s SMEs and local guides.

### **6\. Reviews & Ratings**

* **Verified Check-ins or Orders**: Ensures reviews come from real customers.

* **Photo & Text Reviews**: Customers can share images and detailed feedback.

* **Merchant Replies**: Businesses can respond publicly or privately.

* **Category-Specific Ratings**: Quality, value, service, and experience.

### **7\. Itinerary & Trip Planner**

* **AI-Powered Itinerary Suggestions**: Automatically recommend and design travel routes that highlight Visit Rwanda experiences, community tours, and eco-tourism trails.

* **Dynamic Itineraries**: Users can create multi-day travel plans combining hotels, restaurants, events, and tour packages.

* **Smart Scheduling**: Auto-arrange stops and activities based on location proximity and opening hours.

* **Collaborative Planning**: Share and co-edit itineraries with friends or travel companions.

* **Integration with Bookings**: Sync hotel stays, restaurant reservations, and event tickets directly into the itinerary.

* **Offline Access**: Download itineraries for use without internet.

* **Cost & Duration Estimates**: Auto-calculate total trip cost and time.

* **Guided Suggestions**: Recommend nearby attractions, dining, and activities.

### **8\. Community & Engagement**

* **Local Guides & Tour Operators**: Certified guides and operators can showcase verified experiences, cultural trails, and eco-tours.

* **Live Travel Stories**: Enable guides and travelers to post live stories, photos, or short videos promoting authentic Rwandan culture and destinations.

* **User Feed**: Posts, check-ins, reviews, and shared experiences.

* **Favorites & Wishlists**: Save places, products, or services.

* **Referral Program**: Invite friends, earn Zoea Points.
